Welcome Back!

Your ultimate learning companion for managing academic and professional development. The Personal Course Organizer is designed to help you streamline your educational journey by centralizing all your courses, tracking progress, and maintaining comprehensive study resources.

Whether you're juggling university subjects, pursuing online certifications, or developing new skills, this tool provides a unified platform to manage everything in one convenient location. Stay organized, track your achievements, and never lose important course material again.

What Can You Do Here?

📊 Track Progress

Log all the courses you're currently studying and monitor their status with ease. Mark courses as Completed, In Progress, or New to keep a clear picture of your academic workload and achievements.

💳 Organize Credits

Calculate your total credit hours earned and plan your upcoming semesters efficiently. Get an overview of your academic standing and make informed decisions about course selection.

📚 Study Notes & Resources

Keep all your syllabus links, important notes, and study materials organized in one place. Never search through folders again – access everything you need right from your course profile.
